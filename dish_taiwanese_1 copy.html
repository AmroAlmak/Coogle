Green Onion Pancake

Ingredients

▢2 cup unshifted all purpose flour , around 300g
▢¼ cup room temperature water
▢1 tsp. salt
▢1 tbsp. vegetable cooking oil

Description

▢1.Make the dough, mix salt with all purpose flour.
▢2. Cover the rest for 20 to 30 minutes.
▢3.Take one small ball out, and roll it out to a rectangle around 10cm wide and 8cm high. Brush some oil
▢4.Then roll out the pancake into a large round circle.
▢5.Pan-friying for 1 to 2 minutes.
