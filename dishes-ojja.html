Ojja

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp of olive oil
  • 2 tsp of minced garlic
  • 1/2 Poblano pepper, diced
  • 1 tbsp of tomato paste
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 Roma tomatoes, diced
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/2 tsp caraway
  • 1 tsp Harissa (depends on the spiciness of the harissa)
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • French bread
  • Diced green onion (optional)
  • Diced green onion (optional)

Description

  1. In a medium size skillet over medium heat add oil, garlic, onion and pepper.
  2. Sauté the veggies until they are a little soft, then add tomatoes.
  3. In a small bowl, mix the tomato paste, harissa, caraway, and salt and pepper with the water and add them to the skillet.
  4. Bring it to a simmer and crack your eggs into the skillet. You can mix the eggs in the sauce or leave them whole – it depends on how you like them!
  5. Cook it until you get your desired eggs and remove from heat.
  6. Garnish with some parsley and green onion.
  7. Serve them with the French bread and enjoy!
