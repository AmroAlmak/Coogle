braised Pork Rice

Ingredients

1 lb skin-on pork belly
2 teaspoons oil
0.9 oz. rock sugar
1 small onion or a couple of shallots
8 shiitake mushrooms
1/4 cup shaoxing wine
3 tablespoons light soy sauce
2 tablespoons dark soy sauce
2 cups water
4 hardboiled eggs (peeled, optional)

Description

1. Boil the chopped pork belly for 1 minute. Drain, rinse clean of any scum, and set aside.
2.Heat the oil in a wok stir-fry for another couple minutes.
3.Add the blanched pork, shaoxing wine, light soy sauce, dark soy sauce and water.
4. To finish the dish, remove the spice packet and turn up the heat to medium high.
