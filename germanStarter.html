Potato soup with sausages

Ingredients

  • 1 kg potatoes mainly firm boiled
  • 1 leek
  • 2-3 carrots
  • 1 onion
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 25 g frying fat or butter
  • 1200 ml vegetable broth
  • 100 ml sweet cream
  • parsley
  • 60 g bacon cubes
  • 3 sausages
  • Â½ tsp marjoram
  • salt pepper, nutmeg

Description

1. Peel potatoes, carrots, onion and garlic. Clean the leeks thoroughly. Chop the vegetables, finely chop the onion and garlic. Saute everything together in fat in a saucepan.

2. Add broth and cook for 30 minutes. After 15 minutes season with marjoram, grated nutmeg, salt and pepper.

3. Fry the bacon in a small pan. Cut the sausages into slices. Add to the potato soup together with the cream.

4. Simmer for another 5 min until the sausages are heated. Garnish with chopped parsley and serve.
