Bambalouni

Ingredients

  • Flour (250g)
  • Dried yeast (1tsp)
  • Warm water (150ml-1cup)
  • Salt (1tsp)
  • Sugar (to dip your bambalouni inside of it)
  • Oil (1L to deep fry with)

Description

  1. Mix warm water with the yeast and let it rest for 10 minutes
  2. Add to flour and salt
  3. Keep mixing and kneading the dough until you get a smooth ball (add water if needed)
  4. Cover it and let it rest for 4 hours in a warm place
  5. Heat the oil, and form circles with your dough in the frying pan.
  6. Your Bambalounis should be bronzed! Dip them in sugar or sprinkle them with it on top
You’re done! You’re now ready to eat your magical sweet rings! Tip: Eat your Bambalounis while drinking coffee or tea!
