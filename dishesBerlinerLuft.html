Berliner Luft

Ingredients

  • 250 g raspberries
  • 250 ml apple juice
  • 150 g sugar
  • 4 sheets of gelatine
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 package vanilla sugar
  • 1 lemon

Description

1. Soak the gelatin sheets in cold water for 10 minutes.

2. Separate the eggs and beat the egg yolks with 125 g sugar until foamy. Finely grate the zest of the lemon and stir into the egg yolk mixture together with the juice of the lemon.

3. Warm the apple juice slightly in a saucepan, then squeeze out the gelatine and dissolve it in the apple juice. After it has cooled down a bit, stir the apple juice into the egg yolk mixture.

4. Put the cream in the refrigerator for 45 minutes until it is slightly firm. Then stir again until smooth. Beat the egg whites until stiff, swirling in the vanilla sugar.

5. Fold the beaten egg whites loosely into the cream.

6. Place the starter rings each on a plate and pour the Berliner Luft cream evenly into them.

7. Place the dessert in the refrigerator for another 75 minutes. Set aside 8 raspberries, puree the rest with 25 g sugar.

8. Spread the raspberry puree evenly on the plates, then loosen and lift off the starter rings with a knife. Decorate the four Berliner Luft desserts with 2 raspberries each and serve.
